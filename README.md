# USA's Favorite Travel Destinations - Excel Dashboard

## Overview
Analysis of 31 years of US international outbound travel data, exploring passenger volumes, top destinations, seasonal trends, and year-over-year shifts in travel behaviour.

## Dataset
- **Source:** US Department of Transportation / Bureau of Transportation Statistics
- **Period:** 1990 - 2021
- **Scope:** 46 international destinations, 4 billion+ total passengers

## Key Findings
- Mexico, Canada, and the UK consistently dominate US outbound travel, accounting for ~43% of all passengers in 2020
- By 2021, Mexico, Dominican Republic, and Colombia had risen to account for 56% of total travel, a notable post-COVID shift in destination preference
- Q1 and Q2 account for the highest quarterly passenger volumes at 980M and 991M respectively
- Average monthly passenger volume sits at 349M, peaking in July at 410M

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel - data cleaning, pivot tables, and dashboard visualisation
